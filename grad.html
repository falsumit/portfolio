Graduate Thesis

London NFL Expansion Team

Branding, Print

The National Football League has been rumored for years to be interested in expanding into the European market. I took this premise as an opportunity to build a brand from the ground up utilizing market research and audience feedback. The team and brand went through many different iterations based on interviews with football fans in London as well as peer reviews.

Final deliverables included a full brand style guide, uniforms, banners, tickets, memorabilia, and various other promotional material.
