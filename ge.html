Global Emissionairy and Chamberlain Contractors

Sustainable Concrete and Commercial Paving Branding and Web Design

Global Emmissionairy (GE) is an organization that promotes the use of recycled materials and sustainable installation within the pavement industry. The logo incorporates cues from common recycling iconography to emphasize the environmental focus of the brand. The website uses imagery and an earth-tone palette to demonstrate the real-world impact of GE's technology.

Chamberlain Contractors is a Maryland-based paving service that specializes in commercial parking lots. They are a sister company of Global Emmisionairy and utilize renewable resources and sustainable practices. We followed the same principles with Chamberlain's website, using imagery with supplemental text to showcase the client's work.
