Global Emissionairy

↳ Paving Brand and Web

Brand Identity, Interface Design

Global Emmissionairy is an organization that promotes the use of recycled materials and sustainable installation within the pavement industry.

The logo incorporates cues from common recycling iconography to emphasize the environmental focus of the brand. We paired the logo with an informal geometric word mark and lush earth color palette to reinforce the brand's values.

The website places a heavy emphasis on imagery with supplementary text to showcase the real-world impact of Global Emissionairy's technology.

  • Client: Global Emissionairy
  • Agency: King Cow Interactive
