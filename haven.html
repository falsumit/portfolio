Haven Well Within

A Wellness-Minded Fashion Brand

Branding

Haven Well Within is a female home and fashion brand with a focus on wellness and comfort.

We had a pretty clear idea of the client's goals from the start, so the creative process ended up being quick, with only minor iterations based on client feedback. Early logotypes were a mix of flowing lines and grotesk sans serif lettering. We paired it with a warm earth-tone color palette to emphasize the mix of fashion and nature.

Further iterations of the logotype removed the flowing lines and eventually landed on a mixture of upper and lower case lettering that combines grotesk and geometric cues. We simplified the earth-inspired color palette, resulting in a combination of a calming tea green and a lush forest green.

  • Client: The Talbots, Inc.
  • Agency: Graj and Gustavsen
