↳ Women's Fashion Brand

Brand Identity

Haven Well Within is a female home and fashion brand focused on wellness.

We had a pretty clear idea of the client's goals from the start, so the creative process ended up being quick, with only minor iterations based on client feedback. Early logotypes combined flowing lines and grotesk sans serif lettering. We paired it with a warm earth-tone color palette to emphasize the mix of fashion and nature.

Further iterations of the logotype removed the flowing lines and eventually landed on a mixture of upper and lower case lettering that combines grotesk and geometric cues. We simplified the earth-tone color palette, resulting in a combination of a calm tea green and a lush forest green.

  • Client: The Talbots, Inc.
  • Agency: Graj and Gustavsen
