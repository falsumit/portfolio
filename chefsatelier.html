Chef's Atelier

Branding and Product Design

Chefs Atelier is a home and cookware brand owned by Ross Stores, Inc., providing everything from frying pans to food storage.

Designed in conjunction with Manhattan-based firm Graj + Gustavsen, our aim was to create an upbeat, family-oriented brand that would appeal to a wide range of ages and cooking skill levels - from novice to expert.
