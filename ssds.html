Silver Spring Day School

Preschool Rebrand and Web Redesign

Branding, UI/UX, Web Design

Silver Spring Day School (SSDS) is a parent participatory preschool located in Silver Spring, Maryland. Their philosophy centers around active learning through community, discovery, and exploration. We wanted to communicate a sense of positivity while emphasizing the people in the SSDS community, so the new art direction leans heavily on soft, happy colors and participant photography.

For the website, our goal was to create a function-first resource hub for current and potential participants. We utilized signposting via large color block grids and bold typography to counterbalance all the photography. The end result is a site that feels vibrant and dynamic, yet easy to navigate.
