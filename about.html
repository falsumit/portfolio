About

Hello!

I'm Brooks Clarke, a graphic designer from Maryland. Sadly, I failed to inherit my state's obsession with crab cakes, but I do love Old Bay on everything.

In my experience freelancing and contracting, I've had the opportunity to create brands and websites for a wide range of clients. Visual storytelling is at the heart of everything I create, and is something I believe is instrumental in helping people connect with each other.

I love typography and use it as a pillar in conveying mood and emotion. I believe that type is something that everyone can connect with, and I take great joy in being able to share that passion with people.
